Investigation over Time

To investigate the changes over time through inferential statistics, logistic regressions were performed for all items. Year was investigated as an ordinal predictor (not a continuous variable), with the year 2022 and 2023 both compared to the baseline from the first wave in 2021. The responses were dichotomized for the outcome into agree/disagree and often/not often (with “sometimes” being coded as “not often”), respectively. Missing and “don’t know” responses were filtered out a-priori. McFadden’s R2 was calculated post-hoc from the model output.
