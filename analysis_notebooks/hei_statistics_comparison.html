In 2021, the number of self-identified students among the survey respondents amount to 0.5% of all students according to the Ukraine State Statistics Service. Self-identified staff among the survey respondents make up 3.2% of all recorded staff. In 2022, those numbers lay at 0.6% and 2.6%; in 2023 at 0.3% and 2.3%. Response rates among staff were continuously higher compared to students and highest in 2021. Response rate in students was highest in 2022.

Of all Ukrainian students, 53.8% were women in 2021. Within the student survey respondents, 66.9% reported to be women in 2021, signalling that women are represented over-proportionately among students in this sample. In 2022, 50.4% total compare to a proportion of 59.8% in the sample, in 2023 numbers lay at 46.6% and 61.6%, repeating the pattern of a higher proportion of women in this sample than within the overall student population.
